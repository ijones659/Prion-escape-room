KEY CONCEPTS MASTERED

  • DWI-MRI detects water diffusion changes from dying cells, cortical ribboning and basal ganglia hyperintensity are near-pathognomonic for CJD
  • Prions propagate by contact: PrPSc (β-sheet) physically templates PrPC (α-helix) into its misfolded shape, no DNA, no RNA, no genetic code
  • Disease progression: silent PrPSc seeding → synaptic failure → spongiform vacuolation → neuroinflammation → neuronal death → clinical collapse
  • Fatal Familial Insomnia: D178N PRNP + codon 129 Met/Met → selective thalamic loss → untreatable insomnia and autonomic failure (not primarily dementia)
  • Prions have no nucleic acid, UV, nucleases, and standard 121°C autoclaving all fail because they target things prions don't have
  • Brain region mapping: ataxia→cerebellum, dementia→cortex, insomnia→thalamus, personality change→frontal lobe, visual loss→occipital cortex
  • CDC/WHO decontamination: 1N NaOH 60 min (disrupts β-sheet bonds) + 134°C autoclave 18 min. Both steps. 121°C alone is explicitly insufficient.
